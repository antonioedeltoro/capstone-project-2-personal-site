About Me

I am a talented web developer with a knack for innovative designs and technical expertise, dedicated to creating captivating digital experiences. With a strong foundation in both front-end and back-end development, I excel in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, particularly with frameworks like React. Specializing in the MERN stack—MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js—I build robust, full-stack applications. My React proficiency ensures dynamic, responsive user interfaces, while my skills in Node.js and Express guarantee efficient server-side operations. Seamlessly managing data with MongoDB, I create scalable web applications that meet diverse needs.

In addition to my technical prowess, I have a keen eye for design, utilizing tools like Figma, Photoshop, and Illustrator to craft visually appealing, user-friendly websites. My deep understanding of UX principles ensures intuitive interfaces and a smooth user experience. Collaborative and communicative, I thrive in team environments, working closely with designers, developers, and stakeholders to deliver projects on time. I am constantly learning and staying updated with industry trends, incorporating new techniques and tools to remain at the forefront of web development.
